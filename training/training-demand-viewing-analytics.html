Training on Demand: Viewing Analytics Data

Watching the Viewing Analytics Data training course gives you the skills to use Google Analytics to see what viewers are doing in your Brightcove Beacon apps. You learn how to view Brightcove supplied dashboards/reports which are added to your Google Analytics account. In addition, you get an introduction on how to use dimensions and a metic to build your own dashboards.

There is a logical order to the videos in this course. So it is best to watch the videos in order of their appearance below.
