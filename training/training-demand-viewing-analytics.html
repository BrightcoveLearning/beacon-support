Training on Demand: Viewing Analytics Data

Viewing the Designing the Application Layout training course gives you the skills to use Google Analytics to see what viewers are doing in your Brightcove Beacon apps. You learn how to view Brightcove supplied dashboards/reports added to your Google Analytics account. In addition, you see an introduction on how to use dimensions and a metic to build your own dashboards.
