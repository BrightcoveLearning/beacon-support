Introduction

The Evergent backoffice system provides services to help you manage your business. In this topic, we will cover two of these services:

  • The automated emailer
  • The coupon manager

Emailer

The emailer allows you to create templates and schedules for various system-generated emails. The sections that follow will detail the emailer operations.

Message types

Evergent supports the following message types:

Scheduling
When the batch jobs are scheduled in the system admin, an email is sent to an admin.
Credit Card Expiry Reminder
When the "Credit Card Expiry Reminder" checkbox is selected at the business level then the reminder email is sent to the customers when their credit card is about to expire (the email is usually sent a month before the card expires). In this case the system will use the email template message type "credit card expiry reminder".
Bulk Emails
If a business wants to schedule a promotional email and specify a future time to execute, then the bulk email message type is used. These types of bulk emails can be sent to a business unit subscriber, specific market area subscriber, or a specific service.
Credit Card Transaction
When the credit card payment transaction is done by a subscriber, the transaction details will be sent via email to the customer using this email template.
Event Message
Event message type email templates will be used to define an email template and cross-map them to the event category, which gives the ability to trigger email notifications to subscribers once the event is recorded at the account level.
Adhoc
The adhoc message type is to send the individual messages not linking with any of the event category mapping.

Emailers

The Emailers tab is used to add/edit the email templates and schedule emails for the Business Units.

Adding a new emailer

To add a new emailer, do the following:

  1. On the Emailers screen of a Business Unit, click the Add () icon.
  2. In the Basic Details section, enter the required details, as follows:
    1. On the Title field, enter the title for the email.
    2. On the Email Subject field, type the subject of the email.
    3. Select the message type that you will define an email message from the Message Type drop-down list.
    4. In the Admin Email field, enter the email ID that you want the email to be sent from.
    5. In the Admin Name field, enter the Admin name.
    6. Select the Event Category from the provided drop-down list.
    7. Enter the email ID of the persons you want the email to be sent to in the CC and BCC fields.
      Note: You can specify multiple email IDs in the CC and BCC fields by separating each email ID with a comma.
  3. Select Plain Text or HTML to send the email in the selected format.
  4. In the Email/Newsletter Message section, in the Email Body text box, enter the message template that you want the message to be displayed on the customer’s email.
  5. Click Preview beside the text box area to view the email message in the Plain Text or HTML format.
  6. Click the Create button.

Updating an emailer

  1. Navigate to the Emailer screen of a Business Unit.
  2. In the List of Email Templates section, click the email template that you want to edit.
  3. The Update Emailer screen appears, make the necessary changes in the available fields, and click Update.
Note that you cannot modify the Message Type and Event Category fields on the Update Emailer screen.

Schedule emails

You can schedule the emails to send them later. This screen is to schedule the bulk emails on a specified time basis or daily for the DMA or Service.

Scheduling an email

  1. On the Emailers screen, on the Email Templates tab, click the message type email as Bulk Emails.
  2. Click the Schedule Emails tab. The list of scheduled emails appears.
  3. To schedule an email, click the Add () icon to open the Schedule Email dialog.
  4. Enter the email ID in the Send Mail To field.
  5. Click the Time To Execute field to select the schedule date and time.
  6. To schedule emails daily on the selected date and time, select the Daily Schedule checkbox.

Deleting a scheduled email

  1. Click the Email Templates tab under the Emailers tab from the left menu bar.
  2. Click a Bulk Email (Message Type) that you want to remove.
  3. The Update Emailer screen appears.
  4. Click the Schedule Emails tab.
  5. Click the Delete () icon in the Actions column to delete the scheduled email from the List of Scheduled Emails.

Coupon generation

Coupons generation permission is based on the User’s BU Group Permission. Logged in users having this permission can generate and edit the Coupons.

Adding a new coupon/voucher

  1. Click the Coupons from the left menu.
  2. On the Coupons screen of a Business Unit, click the Add () icon. The Coupons default screen appears.
  3. Click Add () icon for generating the Coupon.
  4. The New Coupon/Voucher generation screen appears as shown below.
  5. Enter information in the fields as follows:
    1. Coupon Prefix: Prefix of the Coupon (ex: TestC, Hal, CD, Friday, etc.)
    2. Batch Name: Name of the Batch for the Coupons (Ex: Test Coupon, Christmas, etc.)
    3. Retailer: Coupons can be generated on the Retailer selection like (AT&T, Vodafone, Verizon, Idea, etc.)
    4. Starting Date: The date from when the Coupon is available for the user
    5. Expiration Date: Users can define the Day the Coupon Expires
    6. Coupon Requestor: Indicates the name of the requestor who has requested the coupons
    7. Credited as Wallet Top-up:
      • For generating the Coupon for wallet top-up, click the toggle switch to enable and enter Amount
      • For generating the Coupon as a voucher, click the toggle switch to disable it
    8. Discount Type: Define the Coupon Type – e.g., Free, Flat, Flat Off, Percentage, Percentage-Off, and Validity Extension
    9. Amount/Percentage: For the type selected, the Amount/Percentage field appears
    10. Period & Duration: The Period of Coupon applied can be number of days or number of months, as the respective selection of period the Durations of the Coupon implies
    11. Make Prefix as Coupon Code: When you check this checkbox, the Prefix will be saved as Coupon Code
    12. No. of Coupons: The user can provide the Number of Coupons generated for the Batch. In the Case of "Prefix as Coupon Code," the No. of Coupons will be "1"
    13. Coupon Priority: Used to set the generic coupon display on a priority order basis as generated in the business and mapped to a subscription and offer type
    14. Coupon Length: Length of the coupon code you want to be generated
    15. Multiple Redemptions: If this toggle switch is selected, the user can define the Maximum Redemptions for a Coupon
    16. Maximum Redemptions: Allows the No. of Redemptions for a Coupon
    17. Multiple Redemptions by Single User: checkbox allows customers to redeem a single Coupon on their account for No. of Maximum redemptions by Single User
    18. Multiple Redemptions by Single User: checkbox allows customers to redeem a single Coupon on their account for No. of Maximum redemptions by Single User
    19. Case Sensitive: Check the checkbox if you want to generate coupon codes in Capital letters
    20. Is Gift: If the checkbox is selected, the Coupon can be given to others
    21. Inheritable: If the checkbox is selected, the Coupon can be inheritable
    22. Is All Items Mandatory: If this checkbox is selected, then all the items are mandatory
    23. Skip Payment Method: If the checkbox is selected, then the payment method will be skipped after applying the Coupon
    24. Coupon Description: Enter the coupon description
    25. Campaign Info: Enter the campaign Info details
    26. Success Message: Enter the success message that is to be displayed to the user.
    27. Warning Message: Enter the warning message that is to be displayed to the user
    28. Applicable Products: User can select the no. of applicable products to the Coupon (Ex: Flat 30% OFF Coupon for product Neon)
    29. Applicable Packages: The user can select the no. of application packages to the Coupons
    30. Offer Types: The user can select the offer types for the Coupons
    31. Applicable Payment Modes: The user can select the applicable payment modes for the Coupon
  6. Click the Create Coupon button to generate coupons (the generation time of coupons also depends on the No. of Coupons the user generates).
  7. Once Coupons are generated, Coupons Generated Successfully message is displayed:

Coupon search

Search the coupons by using the various options. To search the coupons, do the following:

  1. On the Coupons screen, enter the field value to search by Batch Name, Prefix, Coupon Code, Status, Expiration Date From/To.
  2. Click the Search button. The coupon search results are displayed, as shown below.
  3. Click the Enable Advance Search toggle switch to enable the advance search.
  4. You can search with coupon batch for the Applicable Products and Applicable Payment Modes listed in the lists. To search with coupon batch, click the Coupon Batch Search button.
  5. The searched coupons are displayed in the Search Result list, as shown below.
