Introduction

To get to a user's finance and billing history, you will need to login to the Evergent CRM system and locate the account you want to work on. See Locating Accounts in the Evergent CRM for details.

The Billing and Finance tab of the user account information is used to view the Billing and Financial history of your viewers. You can issue Payments, Adjustments, and Refunds to your customers from the Billing and Finance tab.

The following are the functionalities that can be provisioned:

  • Running Balance
  • Invoices
  • Payments
  • Adjustments
  • Refunds

Running Balance

Running balance is a dynamic ledger balance that shows all the credit and debit transactions of that user account based on the selected range with the latest record

Make a payment

This module is used to make payment for a user account by an existing payment method and a new payment method. To make a payment, do the following:

  1. Click the Running Balance hyperlink under the Billing and Finance tab.
  2. Click the Make a Payment button.
  3. Select the payment mode that you want to make a payment.
  4. Enter the card number in the Card Number field.
  5. Enter the type of card in the Card Type field.
  6. Enter the name of a cardholder in the Name on Card field.
  7. Enter the card expiry date in the Expiry (MM/YYYY) field.
  8. Enter the security code in the Security Code field.
  9. Enter the required amount in the Amount field.
  10. Click the Make Payment button. The payment line-item will be added to Payment History.

Adjustments

This module is a transaction that provides the payment corrections of a customer account payment entry. To make the payment adjustments to the account, do the following:

  1. Click the Running Balance hyperlink under the Billing and Finance tab.
  2. Click the Make an Adjustment button.
  3. Select the Adjustment Type from the drop-down list.
  4. Enter the amount that you want to adjust in the Amount field
  5. Select the reason for the payment adjustments from the Select Reason drop-down list.
  6. Select the invoice from the Given-on Invoice drop-down list for which you want to make the adjustments.
  7. Select the Outage Start Date and Outage End Date from the calendar.
  8. Type the comments in the Comment field.
  9. Click the Done button. The adjustments are added in the List of Adjustments section.

Write-off

The Write-Off button in the Running Balance of the respective accounts will get displayed only when the balance due for the account is positive, i.e. the customer must pay the amount. When clicking on the Write Off button, if the customer has paid the amount and add a negative adjustment amount to make the balance due to zero.

Invoices

The invoices section shows all the general invoices created for an account. It shows in detail for each invoice.

Payments

You can create a new payment and view the existing customer payment history, which provides a record of credit card details, ordered product Id, the payment received date, amount, status, along with the option to refund the payment to the customer.

Refunding the Amount

To refund the amount, do the following:

  1. Click the Payments hyperlink under the Billing and Finance tab.
  2. On the Payment History screen, click the Refund button in the Actions column heading that you want to refund for the account.
  3. A dialog box, “Are you sure?” appears as shown below.
  4. Select a reason for the refund in the Select Reason drop-down list.
  5. Type the comments for payment refund in the Comments box.
  6. Click the Yes, Refund button to provide a refund, or else click Go Back to discard the changes.

Adjustments

This module is a transaction that provides the payment corrections of a customer account payment entry.

Making a payment adjustment

To make payment adjustments, do the following:

  1. Click the Adjustments hyperlink under the Billing and Finance tab.
  2. Click the Make an Adjustment button to adjust payments.
  3. The Adjustments screen is displayed as shown below.
  4. Select the Adjustment Type from the available drop-down list.
  5. Enter the amount that you want to adjust in the Amount field.
  6. Select Reason and Given on Invoice from the available drop-down list.
  7. Select Outage Start Date and Outage End Date.
  8. Enter the comments for adjustments in the Comment box.
  9. Click Done for providing the adjustment or else click Go Back.

Refunds

Refunds are calculated, whenever a service is removed or disconnected. From the disconnected/removed date, it will refund the amount for whatever duration it is billed.

Add Refunds

To add refunds to the user account, do the following:

  1. Click the Refunds hyperlink under the Billing and Finance tab.
  2. Click the Refunds Add icon () to issue a refund to a customer.
  3. The following are the refund types in the Type of Transaction list:
    • Billed
    • Unbilled
    • All Payments
    • Chargeback

Refund on Billed Transactions

On selecting the Billed transaction type from the Type of Transaction drop-down list box:

  1. Select any one of the invoices from the Select the Invoice drop-down list.
  2. Select any one of the products from the Select Ordered Product drop-down list box.
  3. Enter the price to be charged in the Price Charged field.
  4. Enter the tax amount in the Tax Charged field.
  5. Enter the refund amount in the Amount to Refund field.
  6. Select the reason for refunding the amount in the Reason Code drop-down.
  7. Type the comments in the Comments box.
  8. Click the Add button to apply the refund to the customer, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Refund on Unbilled Transactions

On selecting the Unbilled transaction type from the Type of Transaction drop-down list box:

  1. Select any one of the Products/Packages from the Select Ordered Product drop-down list box.
  2. Enter the price to be charged in the Price Charged field.
  3. Enter the tax amount in the Tax Charged field.
  4. Enter the refund amount in the Amount to Refund field.
  5. By default, the Adjustment checkbox is selected, which indicates that credit adjustment will be added to the refund amount that will be issued, to clear the running balance amount display as zero.
  6. Select the reason for refunding the amount in the Reason Code drop-down.
  7. Type the comments in the Comments box.
  8. Click the Add button to apply the refund to the customer, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Refund on All Payments Transactions

On selecting the All-Payments transaction type from the Type of Transaction drop-down list box:

  1. Enter the tax amount in the Tax Charged field.
  2. Enter the refund amount in the Amount to Refund field.
  3. Enter the Id in the Reconciliation ID field.
  4. Select the reason for refunding the amount in the Reason Code drop-down.
  5. Type the comments in the Comments box.
  6. Click the Add button to apply the refund to the customer, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Refund on Chargeback Transactions

On selecting the Chargeback transaction type from the Type of Transaction drop-down list box:

  1. Enter the tax amount in the Tax Charged field.
  2. Enter the refund amount in the Amount to Refund field.
  3. Enter the Id in the Reconciliation ID field.
  4. Enter the Id in the Transaction ID field.
  5. Select the reason for refunding the amount in the Reason Code drop-down.
  6. Type the comments in the Comments box.
  7. Click the Add button to apply the refund to the customer, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Credits

Credit is what businesses typically provided to a customer when the amount on an invoice or purchase is disputed or incorrect. This might be due to an admin error, or because the customer requires a full or partial refund.

Adding cash credits

To add the cash credits to the user account, do the following:

  1. On the Credits screen, click the Add () icon to add a new credit.
  2. The Add Credits screen appears:
  3. Click the Choose Credit Type drop-down list and select Cash Credit.
  4. Enter the credit amount in the Amount field.
  5. Select the start date and end date in the Start Date and End Date calendar.
  6. Select the reason from the Reason Code drop-down list.
  7. Enter the comments in the Comments box.
  8. Click the Add button to add the cash credit, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Updating cash credits

To update the cash credits of a user account, do the following:

  1. Click the credit record where you want to update the cash credit details on the Credits screen.
  2. The Update Credits screen is displayed as shown below.
  3. Make the necessary changes to the required fields, and click the Update button to save the changes, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Adding service credit

To add the service credit to the user account, do the following:

  1. On the Credits screen, click the Add () icon to add a new service credit.
  2. The Add Credits screen appears.
  3. Click the Choose Credit Type drop-down list and select Service Credit.
  4. Select a product category from the Product Category drop-down list.
  5. Enter the quantity in the Quantity field.
  6. Select the start date in the Start Date and the end date in the End Date calendar.
  7. Select a reason in the Reason Code drop-down list.
  8. Enter the comments in the Comments box.
  9. Click the Add button to add the service credit, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Updating service credits

To update the service credits of a user account, do the following:

  1. Click the service credit record where you want to update the service credit details on the Credits screen.
  2. The Update Credits screen is displayed as shown below.
  3. Make the necessary changes to the required fields, and click the Update button to save the changes, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Adding line-item credits

To add the line-item credit to the user account, do the following:

  1. On the Credits screen, click the Add () icon to add a new line-item credit.
  2. The Add Credits screen appears.
  3. Click the Choose Credit Type drop-down list and select the Line-Item Credit.
  4. elect a type in the Type of Transaction drop-down list.
    • Billed
    • Unbilled
    • Chargeback

Billed

For a billed line-item credit, do the following:

  1. Select an invoice from the Select the Invoice drop-down list.
  2. Select a product from the Ordered Product drop-down list.
  3. Enter the amount in the Price Charged field.
  4. Enter the amount in the Amount to Credit field.
  5. Select a reason in the Reason Code drop-down list.
  6. Enter the comments in the Comments box.
  7. Click the Add button to add the service credit, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Unbilled

For a unbilled line-item credit, do the following:

  1. Select a product from the Ordered Product drop-down list.
  2. Enter the amount in the Price Charged field.
  3. Enter the amount in the Amount to Credit field.
  4. Select a reason in the Reason Code drop-down list.
  5. Enter the comments in the Comments box.
  6. Click the Add button to add the service credit, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Chargeback

For a chargeback line-item credit, do the following:

  1. Enter the amount in the Amount Refunded field.
  2. Enter the amount in the Amount to Credit field.
  3. Select a reason in the Reason Code drop-down list.
  4. Enter the comments in the Comments box.
  5. Click the Add button to add the service credit, else click the Go Back button to cancel.

Updating line-item credits

To update the line item of a user account, do the following:

  1. Click the line-item record you want to update the line item details on the Credits screen to open it.
  2. Make the necessary changes to the required fields and click the Update button to save the changes, else click the Go Back button to cancel.
