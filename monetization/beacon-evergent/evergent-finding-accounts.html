Introduction

The Evergent CRM, integrated with Brightcove Beacon, allows you to manage your viewer accounts, subscriptions, and transactions. This guide covers the use of the CRM.

Login to Account Management

  1. Open a web browser.
  2. In the address bar, enter the application URL as provided by Evergent.
  3. Enter a valid Email/Username and Password.
  4. Click the Sign In button to begin your Evergent session.
  5. Users with access to multiple business units must select the business unit from the available drop down list and click Submit:
  6. Click the Account Management image button on the dashboard home screen to enter the Account Management application:
  7. A pop-up modal dialog will ask you to choose from the following options:
    • Create Account
    • Existing Account
    • Incomplete Orders
  8. Select Existing Account

Account management

The Account Management module is used to maintain customer details, services, orders, billing and financial, etc. to build relationships with current and potential customers of Evergent.

Managing existing accounts

Once you are in the Existing Accounts section, you can search for the account you want to work on.

  1. On the Account Search screen, in the SEARCH ANYTHING section, enter the account related details to search the existing account by Account Type, Business Unit, or Account Number:
  2. To see more search options, enable the Advanced Search Fields toggle switch:
  3. The advanced search options allow you to search for an account using any combination of many search fields:
