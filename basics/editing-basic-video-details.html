Beacon Basics: Editing Basic Video Details

In this topic you will learn how to edit basic video details.

Prerequisites

  • You have uploaded videos to your Brightcove Beacon account

Steps

  1. In Video Cloud Studio, click Media to open the Media module.
  2. Click on a video title to open the video properties page.
  3. The video properties are organized into sections. To edit video properties, click Edit or double-click in a section.
  4. Click Save to save the changes.
