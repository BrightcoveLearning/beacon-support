Video Cloud images

Whenever a video is ingested into Video Cloud two images are automatically created and shown in the video's properties. The two images created are a Poster and a Thumbnail. This is the IMAGES section in Studio in edit mode.

You MUST change your default ingest profile. Follow these steps:
  1. In Studio go to ADMIN â†’ Ingest Profiles.
  2. Click on your current default profile which is labeled with an Uploading default label.
  3. Click the Duplicate button.
  4. Locate where the thumbnail image is defined. The exact JSON will look slightly different depending on the profile you have duplicated.
  5. Alter the width and height to represent the portrait sized image you will be uploading. The maximums, as shown below are width of 2000px and a height of 3000px. Two examples of altered JSON are shown here (remember, the JSON will look slightly different depending on the profile you are updating). 
    < class="language-json">{
  "media_type": "image",
  "format": "jpg",
  "label": "thumbnail",
  "width": 2000,
  "height": 3000,
  "id": "559a8b0ee4b07cbb40be7d23"
}


"images": [{
    "label": "thumbnail",
    "height": 3000,
    "width": 2000
  },
  ...
]
  6. Click Save.
  7. Then click the Make this my default profile link.

The Poster and a Thumbnail are placeholders for the images used in Brightcove Beacon. DO NOT follow the recommendations for the images as shown in Video Cloud Studio! Instead, use these guidelines:

Poster Thumbnail
Landscape image (16:9):
Minimum Width: 1000px Height: 563px
Maximum Width: 4000px Height: 2250px
Poster image (2:3)
Minimum Width: 1000px Height: 1500px
Maximum Width: 2000px Height: 3000px

This means that you will decide on an image that represents your video and take the appropriate screenshots that meet the requirements shown above. You will then upload the two images into Video Cloud.

Corporate logo

You will need to supply your corporate logo for use in the Brightcove Beacon Experience. Follow these guidelines for the image:

Specification Value
Format .png
Aspect Ratio 1:1
Size 1500px x 1500px

Working in Brightcove Beacon

When entering information in Brightcove Beacon, you will be asked for Non-Textual Data. This means images for the assets you are currently creating. These images can be broken down into two groups, those being:

  • Series/Seasons/Live Events
  • Channel Logos

Details for images of the groups are detailed next.

Series/Seasons/Live Events

For series/seasons/live events a page will appear similar to the following (without the big red Xs over the Thumbnail and Wide banner, for which you do not need to supply images):

When you encounter this screen, supply a JPEG image for the Portrait Poster and Landscape Poster matching the requirements below. You DO NOT need to supply images for Thumbnail and Wide banner.

Image Requirements
Portrait Poster Poster image (2:3)
Minimum Width: 1000px Height: 1500px
Maximum Width: 2000px Height: 3000px
Landscape Poster Landscape image (16:9):
Minimum Width: 1000px Height: 563px
Maximum Width: 4000px Height: 2250px
