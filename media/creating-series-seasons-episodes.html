A series is a collection of related videos shown in a particular order, that usually share a common theme. When creating a series, you must define the relationships between the series, its seasons, and the associated episodes.

A season is a group of videos that cover a set period of time. Each series can have one or more seasons.

An episode is an individual video. You can think of this as a specific movie.

Once you have defined your series and seasons, and ingested your episodes, they can be used in a Brightcove Beacon app.

Reviewing series

To view the list of series that have been created, click Media in the left navigation and then click All Series.

The body of the page will display the following:

  • Series image (this is the landscape image)
  • Series name
  • Series ID - System assigned series ID (unique across all Brightcove Beacon accounts)
  • Status
    • - Published - Series can be used in a Brightcove Beacon app
    • - Unpublished - Series cannot be used in a Brightcove Beacon app
  • Created date and time

Creating a series

To create a series, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to Brightcove Beacon.
  2. In the left navigation, click Media.
  3. Click Create Series.
  4. Enter a Name for the series and click Save.
  5. The Overview page will display. Note, as you tab out of a field or press enter, any changes will be saved. The following details can be entered:
    • Name - The identifier of the series; this is not a customer facing name
    • Landscape, Portrait and Ultra Wide Images - Click the Upload new image link to upload an image to use based upon the following specifications:
      • Landscape - 16x9 aspect ration, minimum 2000px wide
      • Portrait - 2x3 aspect ration, minimum 500px wide
      • Ultra Wide - 21x9 aspect ration, minimum 2000px wide
    • Title - Viewer facing name for the series (displayed in Beacon app)
    • Synopsis - Series synopsis (displayed in Beacon app)
    • Description - Series description (displayed in Beacon app)
    • Production Year - The year the series was produced; this information will appear in the the More Info screen
    • Production Country - The country where the series was produced; this information will appear in the the More Info screen
  6. Enter the Cast details (this information will appear in the the More Info screen):
    • Actors
    • Directors
    • Singers
    • Music Composers
    • Song Writers
    • Writers
  7. Click Discovery in the left sidebar to open the Discovery details page.
  8. Enter Tags for the series.
  9. Enter Genres for the series.

The right side of the Overview page provides the ability to add seasons and a series trailer.

Editing a series

To edit a series, follow these steps:

  1. In the left navigation, click Media.
  2. Click All Series.
  3. Locate the series to edit. Click the series title to open the series details.
  4. Make changes as needed. As you tab out of a field or press enter, any changes will be saved.

Deleting a series

To delete a series, follow these steps:

  1. In the left navigation, click Media.
  2. Click All Series.
  3. Locate the series to delete and do one of the following:
    • Check the checkbox next to the series name and then click the delete icon () OR
    • Click the series Title to open the series details and then click Delete.

Adding a series trailer

To add a series trailer, follow these steps:

  1. In the left navigation, click Media.
  2. Click All Series.
  3. Locate the series to add a trailer to. Click the series title to open the series details.
  4. On the right side of the page click Add Trailer.
  5. Click on a video to use for the trailer. The Search field at the top of the dialog can be used to search for a specific video.
  6. Click Select Video.

The selected video will appear in the Series Trailer section.

To select a different trailer, click Replace Trailer.

Creating seasons

To create a season, follow these steps:

  1. In the left navigation, click Media.
  2. Click All Series.
  3. Locate the series to which the season will be added. Click the series title to open the series details.
  4. On the series Overview page, click Create Season.
  5. Enter a Name for the season and click Save.
  6. The Overview page will display. Note, as you tab out of a field or press enter, any changes will be saved. The following details can be entered:
    • Name - The identifier of the season; this is not a customer facing name
    • Season Number - An integer number representing the season sequence in the series.
    • Landscape, Portrait and Ultra Wide Images - Click the Upload new image link to upload an image to use based upon the following specifications:
      • Landscape - 16x9 aspect ration, minimum 2000px wide
      • Portrait - 2x3 aspect ration, minimum 500px wide
      • Ultra Wide - 21x9 aspect ration, minimum 2000px wide
    • Title - Viewer facing name for the season (displayed in Beacon app)
      Note: For the season Title, you can enter a name like Season 1, but if a series has topics for distinct seasons that may be a better choice. For example, a series on the continents where each season focuses on a continent, good names for the seasons would be Europe, Africa, Asia, etc.
    • Synopsis - Season synopsis (displayed in Beacon app)
    • Description - Season description (displayed in Beacon app)
    • Production Year - The year the season was produced; this information will appear in the the More Info screen
    • Production Country - The country where the season was produced; this information will appear in the the More Info screen
  7. Click Discovery in the left sidebar to open the Discovery details page.
  8. Enter Tags for the season.

To return to the seasons page, click the season breadcrumb at the top of the page.

Seasons that have been added to a series will appear on right side of the season Overview page.

Editing and deleting a season

To edit or delete a season, follow these steps:

  1. In the left navigation, click Media.
  2. Click All Series.
  3. Locate the series with the season to edit/delete. Click the series title to open the series details.
  4. Click the season to edit in the Seasons section.
  5. Make changes as needed. As you tab out of a field or press enter, any changes will be saved.

To delete the season, click Delete at the top of the page.

Deleting a season

To delete a season, follow these steps:

  1. In the left navigation, click Media.
  2. Click All Series.
  3. Locate the series to delete and do one of the following:
    • Check the checkbox next to the series name and then click the delete icon () OR
    • Click the series Title to open the series details and then click Delete.

Ingesting episodes

While creating the series and associated seasons is done in Brightcove Beacon, the creation of episodes for the series is done in Video Cloud Studio. You will assign values about the series and seasons in custom fields for each episode video, then ingest those videos into Brightcove Beacon.

Remember, the truth of Brightcove Beacon episode content resides in Video Cloud. Any changes you make to episodes in Brightcove Beacon will be overwritten when you ingest content from Video Cloud.

Prerequisites

Before continuing with the steps below, make sure that you have completed the following:

  1. Log in to Video Cloud Studio.

  2. From the dropdown menu at the top of the page, select UPLOAD.

  3. Upload your episode videos.

  4. From the dropdown menu at the top of the page, select MEDIA.

  5. Update your episode video images as specified in the Images and Their Specifications document.

  6. In the navigation header, click ADMIN and then Video Fields.

    You must be an account administrator to access the ADMIN menu.
  7. Create the required custom fields needed to ingest videos to Brightcove Beacon. For details, see the Creating Video Cloud Custom Fields document.

Steps

To ingest episode videos into Brightcove Beacon, follow these steps:

  1. Return to Video Cloud Studio.
  2. From the dropdown menu at the top of the page, select MEDIA.

  3. Click on the title of one of the episode videos to enter the video's properties page.

  4. Scroll down until you see on the right-hand side the CUSTOM FIELDS section.
  5. Click the Edit button.
  6. Locate the three custom fields that are associated with episodes.
  7. For the episode video, enter the episode number, season number and series name, in the corresponding custom fields.
  8. Be sure to click the Save button after entering the appropriate values for the video.
  9. Repeat entering values in the custom fields for all your episode videos.
  10. Log in to Brightcove Beacon.
  11. Click on the Tools option (wrench icon) in the top menu.

  12. Be sure the Ingestion option is selected on the left menu.

  13. Click the Update Brightcove Videos button to ingest the newly configured episode videos.

  14. To make the videos immediately available in your apps, clear Brightcove Beacon's cache by clicking on the Cache option in the left navigation, then click the Cache Purge button.

    For more details on using the Brightcove Beacon Tools options, see the Using the Tools document.
