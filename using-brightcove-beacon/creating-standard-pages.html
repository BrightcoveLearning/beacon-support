Including Non-Customizable Pages

In this topic, you will learn how to include non-customizable pages in your application design. Examples of non-customizable pages are Search, Settings, Register, etc.

What are the non-customizable pages?

There are some pages which you either must, should or can include that you cannot customize inside Brightcove Beacon. You either choose to include the page or not.
