Define the languages for your apps

Defining the languages you wish to use in your Brightcove Beacon apps is part of the onboarding process. Past the onboarding process, if you choose to start using multiple languages, or wish to add languages, please contact your Brightcove support contact.

What is localized?

There are two parts of the apps that are/can be localized, they are:

  • The app UI itself, which is localized for you
  • Items you create, like pages names and video metadata, for which you must supply the translated terms
