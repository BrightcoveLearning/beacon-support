Overview

A monetization package defines how you can generate revenue from your app, if you choose to do so.

Terms

Here is a list of term and acronym definitions:

Term Definition
AVOD Advertising video on demand
AuthVOD Authenticated video on demand
SVOD Subscription video on demand
TVOD Transaction video on demand

Monetization packages

Here are the packages offered with Brightcove Beacon. Choose the one that best fits your needs.

Plan Description
Free Visitors can view ad free content in this plan without authentication.
AVOD Visitors can view content containing ads in this plan without authentication.
AuthVOD without ads Authenticated users can view ad free content in this plan by signing in.
AuthVOD with ads Authenticated users can view content containing ads in this plan by signing in.
SVOD Subscribers can view ad free content in this plan.
Mixed Subscribers can view content containing ads in this plan.
Rental (TVOD) Authenticated users can rent content in this plan for a limited time.
Purchase (TVOD) Authenticated users can buy content in this plan.
