Introduction

Undoubtedly when you launch your Brightcove Beacon apps you will want to know what viewers are watching. To accomplish this you will use Google Analytics. The data provided to Google Analytics is setup by Brightcove. For instance, on Movies events are dispatched when a movie played and when time watched can be calculated. Therefore, in Google Analytics you can see the "most watched" movies by both how many times viewers played the movie and total time watched.
