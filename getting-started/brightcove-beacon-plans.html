Overview

Brightcove Beacon is available with the following plans:

  • Essential - Experiences only allow branding for the content owner. This is a cost effective plan for the following organizations:

    • Have smaller budgets
    • Don't have significant existing brand recognition
    • New entrants to the market

    • Have no plans or ability to effectively monetize through advertising

  • Advanced - Provides additional features in your apps and the back end

    • Includes an expanded choice of layouts
    • Ideal for media companies and regional broadcasters with established audiences and advertising monetization plans
