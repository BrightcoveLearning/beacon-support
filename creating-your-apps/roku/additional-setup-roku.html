Introduction

Once you have submitted your Brightcove Beacon app to the Roku store, there a few additional steps needed to test your app.

First, you will need to verify your developer email address. When this is completed, you will be able to receive the Roku package in your channel for testing and publishing.

Some additional setup includes linking your Roku device, setting a payment method and adding a channel using a Vanity code.

Verify your developer account

After completing the Roku channel set up, it is important to verify your developer account.

  1. If you haven't already done so, follow the steps as shown here:

    • Submitting Apps to the Roku Store

  2. Under Manage My Channel, select Package Upload. You will be prompted with this screen:

    At the start of the Channel setup, make sure that you use your organization's email address that will be used for developer rights.

  3. From the top navigation, select the Dashboard link.

  4. Select Manage My In-Channel Products.

  5. Click the Add a new product button.
  6. In the Channels field, select the channels where the product will be available for sale.
  7. Complete the form (reference screenshot below the list of steps):
    1. For the Product Category select Video.
    2. For the Product Name, type the name that will be visible for customers. You can add the product name in different languages by clicking on the Add product name in another language button.
    3. For the Product Identifier, type your internal reference Id.
      Notice that the internal reference Id needs to be unique, and you will need it later to add your In-Channel product to the Brightcove Beacon CMS configuration.

    4. For the Purchase type, select the subscription type based on how your customers are billed for your in-channel product. In this example a Monthly Subscription.

      Note that the subscription interval for Roku is different from the other stores. For details, see the Subscription interval support section of the Using a Subscription Package document
    5. For the Price Tier option, select a price tier based on the price of your in-channel product.
  8. In the Trials and offers section, depending on your in-channel product strategy, you can select Free trial and type a number duration in the Trial duration field and select Days or Months from the time filed.
  9. Go to the bottom of the page, check the Cleared for sale box and click the Save button.
  10. After creation, check to be sure your product is visible on the Manage My In-Channel Products page.
    You can create any number of additional in-channel products based on the type of subscriptions you want to offer. Example: yearly or monthly.

Associate your in-channel product to a channel in Brightcove Beacon

Once your in-channel products have been created, go to the Brightcove Beacon platform to associate your new in-channel products to a channel.

  1. In the top menu click the Commerce tab.
  2. From the left menu, select the Packages option.
  3. Click on your package.
  4. From the Package tab, go down to the Price Details section.
  5. In the Price field, type your in-channel product price.
  6. In the Currency field, select the currency type.
  7. In the Roku Store ID, type the identifier ID of your in-channel product created in the Roku Developers Platform.
  8. Go to the bottom of the page and click the Edit Package button to save your changes.
