Introduction

Once you have submitted your Brightcove Beacon app to the Roku store, there a few additional steps needed to test your app.

First, you will need to verify your developer email address. When this is completed, you will be able to receive the Roku package in your channel for testing and publishing.

Some additional setup includes linking your Roku device, setting a payment method and adding a channel using a Vanity code.

Verify your account and link a device

After completing the Roku channel set up, it is important to verify your developer account.

  1. If you haven't already done so, follow the steps to prepare your Roku account:

    • Submitting Apps to the Roku Store

  2. Next, you'll verify your email address.

    Under Manage My Channel, select Package Upload. You will be prompted with this screen:

    At the start of the Channel setup, make sure that you use your organization's email address that will be used for developer rights.

  3. Once you have verified your developer email account, you will be prompted to link your device and add a payment method. If you have already done this, you can skip to the next section.

  4. When linking your Roku device, make sure that your device has the same email registered as the email in the verification steps. For the best results, we recommend that you reset your Roku device.

    Reset Roku Device : Settings -> System -> Advanced System Settings -> Factory Reset

    Helpful links:

    • Link your Roku TV
    • How to set up the Roku Express/Express+

Add channel with a code

You can use a vanity code to add your app channel to your Roku device.

  1. Expand your profile dropdown menu and select My account.

  2. Select the Add channel with a code link.

  3. In the Add channel dialog, do the following:

    1. Enter the channel access code that we provide to you.
    2. Check the I'm not a robot box.
    3. Select Add channel.

  4. Once the channel has been added to your Roku account, follow these steps to see your app on your Roku TV:

    1. Select Settings.
    2. Select System.
    3. Select System Update.

    You app should now be visible for your review.
