Creating an in-channel Product in Roku

Learn how to add an in-channel product in the Roku Developers page.

  1. From the top navigation, click in the profile icon.

  2. In the drop-down menu, click on the Developers option.

  3. From the top navigation, select the Dashboard link.

  4. Select Manage My in-Channel Products.

  5. Click the Add a new product button.
  6. In the Channels field, select the channels where the product will be available for sale.
  7. In the Product Name field, type the name that will be visible for customers.
  8. In the Identifier, type your internal reference number.
  9. For the Purchase Type field, select the subscription type based on how your customers are billed for your in-channel product.
  10. For the Free Trial Period option, depending on your in-channel product strategy, you can select Enabled and type a duration in days or Disabled.
  11. In the Internet Connection Required option, select Yes.
  12. In the Requires Additional Purchase option, select No.
  13. In the Cleared for Sale option, select Yes.
  14. In the Price Tier option, select a price tier based on the price of your in-channel product.
  15. Go to the bottom of the page and click the Create button.
  16. After creation, your product will be visible on the Manage in-channel products page.
    You can create any number of additional in-channel products based on the type of subscriptions you want to offer. Example: yearly, monthly, weekly.

Associate your in-channel Product to a Channel in Brightcove Beacon

Once your in-channel products have been created, go to the Brightcove Beacon platform to associate your new in-channel products to a channel.

  • In the top menu click the Commerce tab.
  • From the left menu, select the Packages option.
  • Click on your package.
  • From the Package tab, go down to the Price Details section.
  • In the Price field, type your in-channel product price.
  • In the Currency field, select the currency type.
  • In the Roku Store ID, type the identifier ID of your in-channel product created in the Roku Developers Platform.
  • Go to the bottom of the page and click the Edit Package button to save your changes.
