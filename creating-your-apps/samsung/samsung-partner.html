Introduction

In order to launch an application outside of the United States or to get access to certain APIs , you need to have a Samsung Apps TV partnership status, and a partner level group.

Here's the partnership request process:

  1. Create a public group. This is required for the partnership request.

  2. Request an offline agreement with the Samsung content manager. In some cases, this requirement can be waived by the content manager.

  3. Once the Samsung content manager approves your request, you will become a Samsung Apps TV partner.

Getting started

To access the Samsung developer pages, follow these steps:

  1. In a browser, navigate to the Samsung Sign in page.

  2. Either sign in with your account or create a new account.

  3. When you register, you need to request a Partner account with Samsung to be able to submit your apps worldwide. Otherwise, you will be limited to a certain location.

Registering your app

Register your app with Samsung.

  1. From the home page, select the Web App Registration button.

  2. You will see a set of steps to complete before you can submit your app.

  3. First, enter an Application Name. This is your app id and is used for management purposes only. The displayed app title will be entered later.

Helpful hints

The following may be helpful when submitting your app to Samsung:

  • If you need to add a new user to your account you can use the Membership > Group Management option to do so.
  • Be very careful to NOT put spaces in front of email addresses as in some cases they will NOT be stripped out and cause failures in the app submission process.
