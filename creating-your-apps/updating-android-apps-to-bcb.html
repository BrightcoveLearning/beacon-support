Introduction

You may have an existing Android app, but you need to update it so it will be part of your Brightcove Beacon family of apps. This document leads you through that process.

Two other documents address app submission procedures in Android. They are:
  • Submitting New Apps to the Android Store - App submission process if you do NOT have an existing Android app.
  • Adding Subscriptions to Android - Adding subscriptions process for both new app submissions and existing apps.
